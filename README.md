✅ Project name: ToyLand


✅ Purpose:A fun and colorful online marketplace designed just for kids’ toys!
Families can explore a wide range of playful items from local toy sellers, discover unique finds, and share their experiences. Users can log in to browse toys, see detailed product information, and leave reviews or ratings to help others choose the perfect toys for their little ones.


✅ live URL: https://b12-a09-toyland.netlify.app

✅ key features: Users can sign in to explore a world of fun toys, check out detailed descriptions, and share their thoughts or ratings to help other families find the perfect playthings for their kids

✅ used npm packages: React Router, Firebase Auth, Tailwindcss, DaysiUi, Swiper slider, etc...
